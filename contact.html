Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical timeline for project completion?

+

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A typical PSA oxygen plant installation takes 4-6 weeks, while modular OT projects may require 8-12 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during consultation.

Do you provide after-sales support and maintenance?

+

Yes, we offer comprehensive after-sales support including annual maintenance contracts, emergency support services, and technical assistance. Our team is available 24/7 for critical issues.

Are your products certified and compliant with medical standards?

+

All our products meet international standards including ISO, CE, and Indian pharmacopeia standards. We provide complete documentation and certification with every installation.
