Our Story

SRB Healthcare stands at the intersection of healthcare excellence and engineering innovation. Founded by Saurabh Thakkar, we have established ourselves as a leading provider of comprehensive medical infrastructure solutions across India.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to bridge the gap between advanced medical requirements and reliable engineering solutions. Today, we pride ourselves on delivering cutting-edge healthcare infrastructure that meets international standards while being tailored to the unique needs of the Indian healthcare sector.

From state-of-the-art oxygen plants to sophisticated modular operation theatres, every solution we provide is backed by years of expertise, technical knowledge, and an unwavering commitment to quality and safety.

Our Mission

To provide healthcare facilities with reliable, efficient, and innovative infrastructure solutions that enhance patient care and operational excellence. We are committed to delivering world-class medical infrastructure that sets new standards in quality and safety.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted name in medical infrastructure solutions, recognized for our technical expertise, innovative approach, and unwavering commitment to improving healthcare delivery through superior engineering solutions.
